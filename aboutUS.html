About Us


The AZooShop company spreads joy among households pets and their breeders through a network of stores with ZooShop breeding supplies. The first Pet Center store was established in 2022 in Trenčín and since then our retail and wholesale network is still growing.

Our goal is to accompany you, our customers, through the entire life cycle your pets, from the moment you decide to get one from a pet to other care, so that you get the most enjoy the joys of life together with your pet. It is also related to development of cooperation with VETINO and veterinary clinics Jaggy. At the same time, you will also find a salon for dogs.
